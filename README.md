e-plantShopping

Welcome to e-plantShopping, a streamlined web application designed for plant enthusiasts to browse and purchase their favorite greenery online.

Project Overview
e-plantShopping is an intuitive e-commerce platform that simplifies the plant-buying experience. Whether you are a seasoned gardener or a first-time "plant parent," our application provides a curated selection of plants, detailed care instructions, and a seamless checkout process to help you grow your indoor jungle. 

Key Features
Curated Catalog: Browse a variety of indoor and outdoor plants.
Shopping Cart: Easily add, remove, and manage your selections.
Responsive Design: Optimized for both desktop and mobile browsing.
